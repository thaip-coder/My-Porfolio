  • About Me

    My full name is Thai Thanh Phan. I was born and raised in Austin, Texas. I attended the University of Texas at San Antonio for pre-pharmacy. Eventually, I discovered that I did not want to pursue a career as a pharmacist. After realizing I didn’t really know what I wanted to do, I went back home and attended Austin Community College to save money. After some deliberation, I started on the path to becoming a nurse. I took the necessary courses, but like pharmacy, found nursing to also be unfit for me. My interests were clearly not aligned with the medical field. After I quit my pursuit of nursing, I decided to just work and figure things out from there. I did mostly contract delivery jobs for various food deliveries as well as package deliveries for Amazon. I enjoyed the freedom of following my own schedule and choosing when I wanted to work. I did this for a few years and felt that I was becoming stagnant. I was no closer to figuring out a career path than I was when I left nursing behind. So, here I am in this program hoping to create a future for myself.
